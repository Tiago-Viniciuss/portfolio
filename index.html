Design is

EVERYTHING .

And you need to understand this!

What convinces you?

Appearence?
Brand?
Functionality?
Usability?
Value message?

There are just some examples of things an ordinary person observes when buying a product or an idea. And a good design is the most complete union of all these elements above.

I know that it was a very simple explanation. But, remember: Design doesn't have to be complicated.
You choose a product on your mind in the first 5 seconds. One of the reasons for your choice is the emotion. We are emotional beings, so we tend, in 90% of the cases, to follow this emotional side (unconsciously). And one of the best ways to convince us to buy an idea is through the emotion it causes. Design causes emotion.
