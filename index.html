Design is

EVERYTHING .

And you need to understand this!

What convinces you?

Appearence? Usability? Brand?Functionality? Value message? There are just some examples of things an ordinary person observes when buying a product or an idea. And a good design is the most complete union of all these elements above.

Choose one:

This is an common case of choice by emotional reason.

" The people smiling, a happy place and the image is attractive. "

You probably didn't choose this image first because:

" The image is blurry, visibility is poor and people are in a bad mood. "

I know, that was a very simple example. But, remember: Design doesn't have to be complicated.
You chose the image on your mind in the first 5 seconds. One of the reasons for your choice is the emotion. We are emotional beings, so we tend, in 90% of the cases, to follow this emotional side (unconsciously). And one of the best ways to convince us to buy an idea is through the emotion it causes. Design causes emotion.
